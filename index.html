Rules of LUDO NEON

  • Player Configurations: The game supports 4, 5, or 6-player board modes. Local seats can be set as Human, Computer AI, or disabled (Off).
  • Board Anatomy:
    • Bases: The circular slots where tokens start.
    • Safe Zones: Squares marked with a Star (★) protect tokens. Multiple players' tokens can sit on a safe zone cell without capturing each other.
    • Home Run Paths: The colored lanes leading directly into the center triangles (Home).
  • Releasing Tokens: By default you must roll a 6 to move a token out of the base. The setup screen can also allow releases on 1 or 6.
  • Capturing: Landing on a cell occupied by a single opponent token (outside a Safe Zone) captures that token, sending it back to their base. Capturing also grants you an extra roll!
  • Consecutive Rolls: You get an extra roll if:
    • You roll a 6 (up to 2 times consecutively; rolling three 6s skips your turn).
    • You capture an opponent's token.
    • One of your tokens reaches the Home center.
  • Entering Home: A token can only enter Home with an exact roll. (e.g., if you are 2 steps away, you must roll a 2).
  • Winning: The first player to get all of their tokens safely into the center Home wins the game.
